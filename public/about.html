About Me

Justin was born and raised in Diamond Bar California. He played various sports throughout High School and is a member of the second graduating at Diamond Ranch High School. He entered the Navy’s Delayed Entry Program in October of 2001 to serve his country after the attacks of 9-11.

Justin Graduated the Navy’s bootcamp in September of 2002 and went on to complete Hospital Corpsman A-School. Hospitalman Recruit Tercero went on to serve with Third Battalion Seventh Marines where he completed three combat tours in Iraq. Hospital Corpsman Third Class Tercero was awarded the Combat Action Ribbon along with various personal and unit awards before transferring to Naval Hospital Sigonella. Justin would go on to serve in various roles within Navy medicine completing a tour in Afghanistan and a West Pac deployment with the USS Essex Amphibious Readiness Group before his retirement in 2023.

Since retiring Justin has found a new passion in website development, bringing his strong military work ethic and his ingrained attention to detail he enjoys bringing projects to life from vision to reality. He completed a course of Study in Web Development instructed by Dr Angela Yu and is a current student at Fresno City College where he studies Computer Information Management with a concentration in web Development.

Justin is married to his beautiful wife Sarah and together they share two children Justin jr and Isabella. In his spare time Justin enjoys traveling and exploring the world. He’s been across Europe, Asia, and through the Pacific Islands. He hopes to one day take his children on a cruise around the world.
